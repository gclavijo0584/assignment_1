Team History

The Florida Panthers were founded in 1993 and made an impact early, reaching the Stanley Cup Final in 1996. After years of rebuilding, they’ve become one of the top teams in the NHL, competing for championships.

Notable Achievements:

  • Stanley Cup Final Appearances: 1996, 2023
  • Division Championships: 2012, 2016, 2021, 2022
  • Notable Players: Pavel Bure, Roberto Luongo, Aleksander Barkov, Matthew Tkachuk

Who is Matthew Tkachuk?

Born December 11, 1997, in Scottsdale, Arizona, Matthew Tkachuk comes from a hockey family—his father, Keith Tkachuk, was an NHL star. He was drafted 6th overall in 2016 by the Calgary Flames before joining the Panthers in 2022.
